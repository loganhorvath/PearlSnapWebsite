Our Story

Our founder, Emmet Felner, started Pearl Snap after realizing the need for a more convenient car washing solution. As a busy professional himself, he understood the value of time and the importance of maintaining a clean vehicle.

Today, our team of certified detailers is committed to providing exceptional service and results that exceed our customers' expectations. We take pride in our work and in helping our clients maintain their vehicles in pristine condition.

Membership FAQ

How do I schedule my services?

Members can use our online booking system or call us directly. Priority scheduling is available for Daily Driver and Longhorn Luxe members.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, all memberships can be canceled anytime with no cancellation fees after the minimum term. Simply contact us 7 days before your next billing cycle.

Do I have to use all my washes each month?

No, you can choose when and how many times you want to book.
